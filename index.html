Everything you need to know about learning German with DeutschMeister

How is this different from other German courses?

DeutschMeister combines structured lessons with cutting-edge technology. Our AI pronunciation coach, interactive exercises, and live tutoring sessions create a comprehensive learning experience you won't find elsewhere.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes sample lessons from all levels (A1-C2), basic vocabulary and grammar exercises, and our blog with learning tips. It's a great way to try our teaching method.

How often are the live tutoring sessions?

Premium members get 2 group sessions per month, while Pro members get 4. You can also book private sessions at any time (additional cost applies).

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no contracts or cancellation fees.

What's the difference between A1, A2, B1, etc.?

These are CEFR (Common European Framework) levels. A1 is absolute beginner, A2 elementary, B1 intermediate, B2 upper intermediate, C1 advanced, and C2 mastery/near-native.
