About Bernadette

"Music is the divine way to tell beautiful, poetic things to the heart."

- Pablo Casals

A Musical Journey

Born in Vienna to a family of musicians, Bernadette König began her musical education at the age of five. By twelve, she was accepted into the prestigious Vienna Conservatory, where she studied under the tutelage of Maestro Gustav Lehmann.

Today, Bernadette is celebrated as one of the most expressive and technically brilliant cellists of her generation, with performances that seamlessly blend classical precision with contemporary sensibility.

Education

Vienna Conservatory, Juilliard School, Royal College of Music

Instruments

1699 Giovanni Grancino Cello "The Aurora"

Career Milestones

2025 - Solo Debut at Carnegie Hall

Performed Dvořák's Cello Concerto to critical acclaim with the New York Philharmonic under the baton of Maestro Andris Nelsons.

2023 - ECHO Klassik Award

Received the prestigious ECHO Klassik Award for "Best Instrumentalist of the Year" for her album "Bach: The Cello Suites."

2021 - Deutsche Grammophon Recording

Signed exclusive recording contract with Deutsche Grammophon, releasing her debut album "Nocturnes."

2018 - Juilliard Graduation

Graduated with honors from The Juilliard School, where she studied with celebrated cellist Joel Krosnick.

Masterclasses & Lessons

Private Instruction

Bernadette offers personalized cello instruction for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced performers seeking competition and audition preparation.

Instruction Options

Individual Lessons (60/90 min)

Tailored one-on-one instruction in technique, repertoire, and performance practice.

Masterclasses (Small Groups)

Ideal for students preparing for competitions or auditions, with peer observation.

Workshop Series

Specialized sessions focusing on Bach interpretation, extended techniques, and more.

Online Lessons

Remote instruction via high-quality video conferencing for international students.

Summer Intensive

Join my annual 5-day summer program with daily private lessons, masterclasses, and performance opportunities.

Youth Program

Special classes for young cellists aged 8-16 with group lessons and fun musical activities.
